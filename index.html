About Our School

Our Story

AIC Leseru Boys School, founded in the year 1983 is a Boarding Boys, County level Secondary school located in Turbo Sub-County, Uasin Gishu County, Kenya.

Under the leadership of Principal Daniel Loponoki assisted by his Deputy Mr.wilson Kimani Misoi, we strive to equip our students for life.

Academic Programs

Sciences

Our science program includes Biology, Chemistry, and Physics with well-equipped laboratories for practical sessions. Students are encouraged to participate in science congresses and exhibitions.

Mathematics

We have a strong mathematics department with dedicated teachers who employ innovative teaching methods to make the subject accessible and enjoyable for all students.

Languages

Our language curriculum includes English and Kiswahili. We emphasize communication skills, literature appreciation, and creative writing.

Humanities

We offer History, Geography, Religious Education, and Business Studies with a focus on critical thinking and real-world applications.

Technical Subjects

Students can pursue Computer Studies, Agriculture, and other technical subjects to develop practical skills relevant to the modern job market.

Co-Curricular Activities

We encourage participation in drama, music, sports, clubs, and societies to foster talent development and leadership skills.
